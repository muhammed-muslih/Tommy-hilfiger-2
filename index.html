ABOUT US

One of the world's leading designer lifestyle brands globally recognized for bringing to life the classic American cool spirit. Tommy Hilfiger markets its products under several brands in order to fully capitalize on its global appeal, as each brand varies in terms of price point, demographic target and distribution.

Tommy Hilfiger employs over 17,000 associates spread across more than 90 countries worldwide. They come from all walks of life and actively promote a diverse culture that champions outspoken individuality and uncompromised creativity.
